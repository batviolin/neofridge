Welcome to Neofridge AI

An inclusive smart refrigerator built to empower visually impaired users — object recognition, freshness checks, hydration monitoring, and personalized diet guidance — all through simple voice and camera interactions.

See Live Demo Explore Features

Core Features

A compact set of features chosen to directly address everyday gaps for visually impaired users.

Object Detection

Point an item at the camera and Neofridge announces what it is — bottles, packets, cans, and more.

Hydration Detection

Detects transparent bottles and estimates water levels as Full, Half, or Low.

Fruit Freshness

Analyzes color and texture to warn users about spoiled or rotten fruits.

Voice-driven Diet

Ask for the day and get a personalized diet plan tailored to health goals.

Impact & Potential

Neofridge is more than a device — it’s a platform for independence. Early pilots have shown strong improvements in daily confidence and kitchen safety for visually impaired participants.

  • Scalable — model updates over-the-air (optional) to improve recognition.
  • Privacy-first — on-device image processing ensures data safety.
  • Customizable — adaptable diet plans for local cuisines.

Roadmap & Future Enhancements

  • Nutritional estimation for detected food items.
  • Smart inventory and expiry-tracking.
  • Mobile caregiver connectivity and remote checks.
  • Multilingual support and regional diets.
  • Lightweight models for offline use.

Technical Architecture

Sensors & Edge

Camera module + microphone, on-device DSP, and hardware-accelerated inference.

AI Models

MobileNet/YOLO-tiny for object detection, CNNs for freshness classification, and heuristic water-level estimation.

UX & Voice

Speech-to-text + local TTS providing audio-first interaction for ease of use.
